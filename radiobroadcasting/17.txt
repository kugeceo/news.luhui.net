张洪科，1962年6月生，大学文化，高级编
辑，现任红河日报社副总编辑，云南省少数民
族新闻工作者协会副会长、红河州新闻工作者
协会副主席。

一个从大山深处走出来的农民的儿子，由
云南省公安学校毕业后，曾穿了9年多的警服 。
1992年10月，弃警从文， 曾经任过屏边县广播
电视局局长、红河人民广播电台台长、红河电
视台台长、 红河州广播电视局副局长。对广
播电视工作的理论研究和实践有一定建树，先
后发表论文50多篇，还有不少获得中央和省级
奖；编辑出版《广播改革与发展对策研究》、
《红河，2005》等书籍，曾两次被评为云南省
广播电视系统先进个人， 一次被评为红河州劳
动模范 。

“摄影只是一种业余爱好，尽管可以促进
身心健康，却不可以当饭吃。”这是为了防止
玩物丧志而经常提醒自己的一句话。

Zhang Hongke (June, 1962 - ), senior editor, Deputy Editor in 
Chief of Honghe Daily , Vice President of the Minorities’ Journalists 
Association of Yunnan Province, Vice chairman of the Journalists 
Association of Honghe Prefecture. 

Coming from deep mountains, a farmer's son, after graduating 
from Yunnan Public Security College, served as policeman for more 
than nine years. October 1992, the author quit the police job, worked 
as director of radio and television bureau of Pingbian County, in the 
next few years, once worked as Director of People’s Radio Station 
of Honghe Prefecture, Director of TV Station of Honghe Prefecture, 
Deputy Director of Broadcasting And Television Bureau of Honghe 
Prefecture. Of theoretical study and practical work in radio and 
television, the author published more than 50 papers, some of them 
won awards both central and provincial level; edited and published 
On Broadcast Strategies reform and development, Honghe, 2005 and 
other books, has twice been named as the advanced individual in 
Yunnan radio and television systems, and once named a model worker 
in Honghe Prefecture. 

"Photography is just a hobby, although could promote physical 
and mental health, but can not do as a career." This sentence always 
be said to remind himself of not losing ambition while being immersed 
in interests. 


序

中共红河州委常委、州委宣传部部长 伍皓

一条河流，由中国云南省大理白族自治州巍山县蜿蜒上千公里，向
南奔驰而来，经过玉溪的元江县后进入红河州，它的上游人们习惯叫元
江，到了红河州境内才叫红河。红河州因红河贯穿全境而得名。它位于
云南省东南部，北连昆明，东接文山，西邻玉溪，南与越南社会主义共
和国接壤，国境线848公里，北回归线横贯东西。 

巍巍哀牢山与滚滚红河水，谱写出红河州瑰丽而浪漫的篇章。在
全州3.2万平方公里的土地上，山峦叠嶂，河流纵横，风光无限。国家
级自然保护区屏边大围山和绿春黄连山，林涛声声，云蒸霞蔚，仿佛是
一个迷离恍惚的梦幻世界；金平的马鞍底蝴蝶谷，小河边，瀑布旁，泉
水叮咚，蝴蝶飞舞，让人心中平添几许诗情画意；石屏异龙湖、蒙自长
桥海、个旧大屯海等地湖光山色，美不胜收。而分布于元阳、红河、
绿春、金平等地、被列入世界文化遗产的哈尼梯田，犹如万道银梯，依
山傍势，镶嵌在哀牢山间，春夏秋冬各显异彩，阴晴朝夕各有不同，
充满了神秘色彩。尤其是冬春季节最有看头。这时梯田灌满水，就像无
数块镜子镶嵌在山间。清晨，云雾笼罩沟壑，白茫茫几十公里，山峦、
梯田、村庄、森林分布其中，不是画胜似画。天气晴朗的日子，朝霞反
射到梯田里，色彩由深渐渐入浅，红色、橘黄色、白色，眨眼功夫就是
另一个样。云开雾散，蓝天呈现在上空，梯田则是一片湛蓝，寂静，唯

美。太阳出来，梯田下方的云雾渐渐升腾，这时云蒸霞蔚，梯田和梯田
边上的村寨时隐时现，似仙境般令人叹为观止。日落时分，晚霞把梯田
映红，天地一色，令人震撼，直到最后一抹阳光消失，梯田才在牧归的
牛铃声陪伴下进入梦乡。

自古“仁者乐山，智者乐水”，山山水水，千百年来总在静心洗
涤芸芸众生的心灵。《光影红河》以红河州州府所在地蒙自市及周边的
屏边县、元阳县、红河县为主，真实地把红河的美丽风光一览无余地呈
现出来，或粗犷或伟岸或柔美，一切都是那样自然，那样纯真，那样妙
曼，为读者诸君提供了难以忘怀的视觉盛宴。

有人说，山川之美，美在千姿百态、气象万千；美在永恒，美在对
智慧生命的启示。让我们满怀喜悦的心情，走进红河这片热土，投入大
自然的怀抱，让生命故事演绎得更舒坦，更充实，更了无遗憾……

Foreword 


By Mr. Wu Hao, Member of the Standing Committee of Red River (Hongue)Prefecture 
& Minister for the Department of Propaganda, Red River Prefecture 

In china, there is a river whose headwaters lie in Weishan Country 
of Dali in Yunnan Province. It meanders almost a thousand kilometers to 
the South and flows into the Red River Prefecture after passing Yuanjiang 
country of Yuxi. The upper reaches of the river are traditionally called the 
Yuan River while the part in the Red River Prefecture is called the Red River 
and the Prefecture is named after this river which runs through it.The Red 
River Prefecture is located in the southeastern area of Yunnan province, with 
Kunming to the north, Wenshan to the east, Yuxi to the west and bordering 
the Socialist Republic of Vietnam to the south, with a boundary line of some 
848 kilometers. The Tropic of Cancer cuts through the Prefecture from east to 
west. 

The Red River Prefecture covers a land area of 32,000 km2,with 
numerous mountains and rivers among which the towering Ailao Mountain 
and the roaring Prefecture River are fine examples of the beauty and 
elegance of the Red River Prefecture. The national Nature Reserves Dawei 
Mountain in Pingbian and Huanglian Mountain in Lüchun seem like mystical 
wonderlands with seas of trees and layers of clouds. Poetic views of butterflies 
dancing around rivers, streams and waterfalls are everywhere in the Ma’andi 
Butterfly Valley in Jinping. There are also beautiful sceneries of water and 
mountains at Yilong Lake in Shiping, Changqiao Sea in Mengzi and Datun Sea 
in Gejiu. The Hani Terrace Fields located in Yuanyang,Honghe, Luchun and 
Jinping Counties have been classified as World Cultural Heritage. These terrace 
fields are like tens of thousands of silver ladders carved on hillsides encased in 
the Ailao Mountain, with changing appearances depending on the weather 
and season. They are full of mystical characteristics, especially in winter and 
spring. During these seasons, the fields are irrigated with water and they look 

like mirrors inlaid in the mountains. In the morning, the raciness is covered 
by clouds and mist. It is completely white for tens of kilometers, enveloping 
mountains, terrace fields, villages and forests. It is not a painting but it is 
better than a painting. On fine days, reflected morning sun rays shine on the 
terrace fields with fading colors; red, orange, white and changing completely 
suddenly. When the clouds disperse, one can see the blue sky above and the 
azure blue, tranquil and beautiful terrace fields rises slowly, hiding the sunshine 
intermittently, and the terrace fields and villages by the sides of the fields appear 
from time to time. It is like in heaven. When the sun is setting, the sky and 
the terrace fields are all overwhelmingly red. Until the last ray disappears, the 
terraces then start their dreams, accompanied by the sounds of bells worn by 
cattle going home. 

There is an old saying which says people with mercy love mountains 
while people with wisdom love waters. Mountains and waters have been 
purifying people’s souls for generations. The photography collection, Light 
and Shades of Red River, faithfully portrays the beautiful scenery in the Red 
River State, especially those of Mengzi city, the capital of the Red River State, 
and the neighboring countries such as Pingbian, Yuanyang and Honghe. It is a 
collection of works which are uninhibited, or imposing or tenderly beautiful, 
all of which are so natural, pure and graceful. It is an unforgettable visual feast 
for the readers. 

It is said that the beauty of nature lies in its diversity, perpetuity, eternity 
and its inspiration for wisdom and life. Let us explore the land of the Red 
River Prefecture with great joy and be embraced by nature. Let us make our 
lift stories more enjoyable, more colorful and more un-regrettable.

后记 Postscript 


人总要有一些业余爱好。有了业余爱好，生活才会充实，心情才会更加舒畅。而

我，斗地主、打麻将不会，唱歌、跳舞不行，琴棋书画不懂。好在多少还晓得一些摄

影，于是，便把它当做了自己的业余爱好，认认真真加以培养。

大概是生在红河，长在红河的缘故，红河秀美的山川田野和那瞬息变换的光影时
时刻刻都在感染着我，诱惑着我。在妻子周锦云的鼎力支持下，只要有时间，我便起早
贪黑，背起相机全身心投身于长桥海、大屯海、芷村、五里冲水库、屏边的大围山、绿
春的黄连山、元阳哈尼梯田等地美丽大自然的怀抱，脚踩大地，头顶蓝天，仰望气势磅
礴的群山，聆听回转吟唱的流水，追逐气象万千的云霞，领略红河如诗如画的美景，呼
吸原野清新空气，感悟四季冷暖的精彩生活，心灵仿佛得到净化的同时，日出日落的光
影、线条与色彩也在我的相机中也留下了难以忘怀的华章。

作为一名摄影爱好者，我决定把其中的一部分整理出版，不仅是一种追忆，更是发
自内心深处、对养育我的这片土地的一种感恩与报答。

此画册的出版，得到了相关领导、朋友及亲属的大力支持。中共红河州委常委、宣
传部部长伍皓先生作序，州政协副主席、州总工会主席普菊红女士、州广播电视局局长
白宏伟先生、红河日报社社长毛杰先生和红河州摄影界的朋友戴云良先生、纳建忠老师
等都先后给予了支持和帮助，在此表示感谢。同时，我还要感谢我的妻子对出版画册的
支持；感谢在澳洲念书的女儿张菡对文字翻译提供的帮助。

People need to have some hobbies to enrich their life and relieve stress. I don’t know how to play poker 
games and Mahjong. I am no good at singing and dancing, and do not possess any of the four Chinese artistic 
skills in instrumental music, chess, literature and painting. I am luck I know something about photography. 
Therefore, I take photography as my hobby and embrace it whole-heartedly. 

Perhaps it is because I was born and raised in the Red River Prefecture; I am always touched and 
attracted by its breathtaking and ever-changing scenery of mountains and rivers. With the support of my wife- 
Zhou Jingyun, I seized every bit of my leisure time travelling through places including Changqiao Sea, Datun 
Sea, Zhi Village, Wulichong Reservoir, Dawei Mountain in Pingbian, Huanglian Mountain in Lüchun and 
Hani Terrace Fields in Yuanyang, with my camera. While in the embrace of the beautiful nature landscape, I 
walked the land under the blue sky, admired the magnificent mountains, listened to the songs of the winding 
waters, ran after the changing clouds, and absorbed the poetic views of the seasons. While my soul was being 
purified, I captured the unforgettable images of sunrise, sunset, lines and colors with my camera. 

As an amateur photographer, I decided to publish part of my works. It is not only a kind of nostalgia, but 
also my expression of heartfelt gratitude and paying back to the land that raised and nurtured me. 

Leaders, friends and relatives have given me great support for the publication of this photography 
collection. I would like to express my deepest appreciation to Mr. Wu Hao, Member of the Standing 
Committee of the Red River Prefecture and Minister for Propaganda for Red River Prefecture who kingly 
wrote the foreword. 

I owe my most sincere gratitude to Ms Pu Juhong, Vice President of the Red River Prefecture Political 
Consultative Committee and President of the Red River Prefecture LaborUnion; Mr. Bai Hongwei, Director 
of the Red River Prefecture Radio and Television Bureau; Mr. Mao Jie, Publisher of Red River Daily, and 
the Red River State photographers Mr. Dai Yunliang and Mr Na Jianzhou for their support and assistance. 

My warmest thanks go to my family. I would like to my wife for all the support that she has given to 
me. She has lost a lot. Without her sacrifice and understanding, it would be impossible for me to realize my 
photography. I also would like to thank my daughter Zhang Han, who is studying in Australia, for her help in 
regard to translation.
